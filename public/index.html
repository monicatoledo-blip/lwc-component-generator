How to Deploy Your Component

Deploy your custom Lightning Web Component directly to Salesforce with just a few clicks.

Step 1: Connect to Salesforce

Click the "Connect to Salesforce" button to securely authenticate with your demo org. You only need to do this once per session.

Step 2: Customize & Deploy

Fill out the form with your desired text, colors, and (for components that use them) images. Once you're ready, click "Deploy to Salesforce". The app will automatically generate the code, upload files to the cloud when applicable, and push everything directly to your org.

For Refinance Offer, set the institution rate label to your brand (e.g. replace “Cumulus” in the default copy). The card uses fixed Cumulus / Salesforce blue (#0176d3) for the header, rates, and bar; only the CTA button color is configurable separately in the form (and in App Builder). All values are static mock data for demos until you wire real data in Salesforce.

Step 3: Add to Your Lightning Page

In App Builder, search the Custom section for the friendly label below (not the technical LWC folder name refinanceOfferLwc). The Refinance Offer component is built for slim columns: the two rate cards stack when the column is narrow. On a Lead (or any) record page, use Lightning App Builder → Custom → search Refinance Offer. After a meta.xml change, redeploy and refresh App Builder if the component does not appear.

  1. Log into your Salesforce org and navigate to the page where you want to add this component (e.g., a specific Account, or your Home page).
  2. Click the Setup Gear Icon ⚙️ in the top right corner and select Edit Page to open the Lightning App Builder.
  3. On the left side of the screen, scroll down to the Custom components section.
  4. Find the component in the Custom section. It will appear with one of these labels (use the builder search box if needed):
    • Unified Profile
    • Agentforce Lead Brief (Custom)
    • Next Best Actions (Custom)
    • Next Best Leads (Custom)
    • Engagement History
    • Refinance Offer
    Drag it onto the page canvas.
  5. Click Save and Activate.

Step 4: Need to make a change? (Re-deploying)

Made a typo or want to swap the image? No problem! Just update the form right here and click "Deploy to Salesforce" again. The deployment will automatically overwrite the existing component in your Salesforce org (as long as you're deploying the same component type).

⚠️ Important: After re-deploying, you MUST remove the old component and re-add it:

  1. Go to your Lightning page in Salesforce
  2. Click the Setup Gear ⚙️ → Edit Page
  3. Remove the old component from the page
  4. Click Save
  5. Edit the page again and re-add the component from Custom section
  6. Click Save and Activate
💡
Pro Tip: Tweak it directly in Salesforce!

You don't always need to re-deploy to make updates. Because your custom component is built with dynamic properties, you can change text, colors, and other configured fields right inside Salesforce! Select your component in App Builder and use the properties panel on the right.
